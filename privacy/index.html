Privacy Policy

I take privacy seriously. Both personally, and as WRCTECH LTD, I’m committed to looking after your personal information, handling it in a responsible manner, and securing it with industry standard administrative, technical and physical safeguards.

This Privacy Notice will be regularly updated to ensure that it continues to comply with the latest regulation and best practice. It was last updated on 24th March 2025.

Visitors to this website

I use a third party service, Google Analytics, to collect standard internet usage information and details of visitor behaviour patterns. I do this to find out things such as the number of visitors to the various parts of the site. This helps me to decide what type of content is most popular (and therefore useful) to visitors, so I can prioritise that.

Members of the public who make enquiries

If you contact me (or WRCTECH LTD) with an enquiry, I’ll will store your details only for as long as necessary to allow me to respond, and for up to 3 months after our last communication. If your query leads to a contract, or other paid work, I may store your details for longer (up to 7 years) in order to comply with auditing and other legal obligations.

Mailing lists

If you sign up to my newsletter, I will store your personal details to facilitate you receiving the newsletter and related emails. You can unsubscribe at any time. I use a third party provider, Buttondown, to manage this process. You can read their Privacy Policy here: https://buttondown.com/legal/privacy.

Suppliers and others to whom I (or WRCTECH LTD) make payments

If I have an obligation to pay you (e.g. following delivery of services or to reimburse expenses) I will collect personal data from you to enable me to complete this transaction. I will store this data in our banking and accounting system, Tide. You can read their Privacy Policy here: https://www.tide.co/terms/privacy/.

IT management systems

I, and WRCTECH LTD, use several systems to manage my IT infrastructure. Personal data of users (predominantely: newsletter subscribers, client, and suppliers) is collected to enable business operations. This data is logged in our accounts held on these systems. This includes:

  • Microsoft – to provide company office suite facilities, including email functionality, and products such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and file storage for day-to-day company activities via OneDrive. Read their Privacy Policy here: https://www.microsoft.com/en-gb/privacy/privacystatement
  • Google – to provide website analytics and, occasionally, file storage where the Mircosoft equivalient is not practical for a client. Read their Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en-US
  • Buttondown – to provide mailing list and newsletter functionality. Read their Privacy Policy here: https://buttondown.com/legal/privacy
  • Tide – to provide banking and accounting services. Read their Privacy Policy here: https://www.tide.co/terms/privacy/

Audit and regulatory requirements

I (WRCTECH LTD) may share any data about the operation of the company, and only where strictly required by law, partners, suppliers, and clients, with the Information Commissioner’s Office, Companies House and other regulatory bodies should this be necessary to complete statutory audit and regulatory requirements.

Your rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) you have rights as an individual data subject which you can exercise in relation to the information we hold about you. Read more about these rights on the ICO’s website.

If you want to make a complaint about the way I (WRCTECH LTD) have processed your personal information, you can contact the ICO as the statutory body which oversees data protection law ico.org.uk/concerns

Privacy notice changes

I will regularly review our privacy notice. Although most changes are likely to be minor, changes may be made from time to time to ensure this policy remains current and responsive to any changes in how WRCTECH LTD receives and protects your personal information. We encourage you to frequently check this page for any changes to our policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
